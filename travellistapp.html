App Overview

The Travel Packing List App is a React-based application designed to help travelers efficiently organize and track the items they need for their trips. Users can add, delete, and mark items as packed, ensuring they don't forget essential belongings. The app provides a seamless experience for planning and managing a packing checklist.

Features Of The App

  • Add items with a name and quantity to the packing list.
  • Mark items as packed/unpacked with a simple checkbox toggle.
  • Delete individual items from the list when no longer needed.
  • Track the percentage of items packed in real-time.
  • Clear all items with a single button for a fresh start.

Challenges

  • Ensuring the app updates the packing status instantly while maintaining performance.
  • Managing state efficiently to handle the addition, deletion, and toggling of items.
  • Providing a simple yet effective UI to make the packing process more user-friendly.
