Chat Stickers Design

BACK

Purpose of this creative work

I've to design a set of original chat stickers (5 stickers) that reflects myself or my business for my graded assignment.

Design Rationale

The concept for my six stickers revolves around an otter who is also a football player. Each sticker showcases a unique expression or scenario, brought to life with a comic art-inspired starburst effect background.

The first sticker features the otter waving hello with its hand raised. In the second sticker, the otter appears sad, with tears streaming down its cheeks and its hands covering its face. For these two stickers, I used a "Desert Sand" color (#D6BFAE) to represent its home kit, much like how football teams have designated home and away kits.

The third sticker captures the otter in a thoughtful pose, pointing a finger to its cheek in a gun-like gesture. Meanwhile, the fourth sticker depicts the otter in a state of shock, with wide, white eyes. For these two stickers, I opted for a "French Gray" color (#B7C4CE) to signify its away kit.

Finally, the sixth sticker showcases the otter as a referee, angrily holding up a red card. This completes the series, with each sticker embodying a distinct personality and role within the football theme.
