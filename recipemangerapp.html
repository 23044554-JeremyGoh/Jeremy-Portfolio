Recipe Manager App

App Overview

Recipe Manager is a React Native app designed for cooking enthusiasts to manage and organize recipes effortlessly. With a clean and intuitive interface, this app allows users to add, edit, and view recipes, as well as calculate the total cooking time for all recipes. It's a perfect companion for anyone who loves cooking and wants to stay organized in the kitchen!

Features Of The App

  • Browse through a categorized list of recipes with details such as name, ingredients, and cooking time.
  • Add new recipes with details like name, ingredients, and cooking time.
  • Modify existing recipes and save updates instantly.
  • Calculate the combined cooking time for all recipes, displayed in hours and minutes.

Challenges

  • Ensuring that cooking times were input correctly and displayed consistently across the app.
  • Managing the shared datasource across screens required careful synchronization to ensure changes were reflected in real time.
